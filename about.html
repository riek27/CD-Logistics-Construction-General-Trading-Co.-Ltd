About Our Company

Building South Sudan's future with integrity, quality, and innovation since 2010.

Our Story & Mission

Founded in 2010 in the heart of Juba, BEL Construction Company LTD began with a vision to transform South Sudan's construction landscape. What started as a small team of dedicated builders has grown into one of the nation's most trusted construction companies.

Our mission is simple yet profound: To build structures that not only meet but exceed expectations, while contributing to South Sudan's development and creating lasting value for our clients and communities.

Our Vision

To be South Sudan's premier construction company, recognized for excellence, innovation, and sustainable building practices.

Our Values

Integrity, quality, safety, innovation, and community engagement guide every project we undertake.
