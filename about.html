About Our Company

Building South Sudan since 2010

Founded in Juba, CD Logistics has grown from a small trading post to a multi-disciplinary construction and logistics leader. We’ve delivered over 50 major projects across the region, combining international expertise with local insight.

Our team of engineers, architects, and logisticians work hand-in-hand to ensure every project exceeds expectations.
