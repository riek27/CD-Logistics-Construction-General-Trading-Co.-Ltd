About CD Logistics

Decades of trust, one project at a time

Company History

Founded in 2012 in Juba, CD Logistics began as a small general trading outfit. Through unwavering commitment to quality and safety, we rapidly grew into one of South Sudan’s most respected construction and logistics firms. We have successfully delivered hospitals, educational institutions, commercial high‑rises, and critical infrastructure — always on time and within budget.

Our Mission

To provide innovative construction and logistics solutions that drive South Sudan’s development, while upholding the highest standards of safety and ethics.

Our Vision

To be the benchmark for engineering excellence in East Africa — a partner of choice for visionary projects.
