About Our Company

Our History: Building South Sudan Since 2010

CD Logistics was founded in Juba by a team of engineers and logistics experts who saw the need for reliable, high-quality construction services in the region. What started as a small general trading company quickly evolved into a full-fledged construction and logistics enterprise. Our first major contract was the renovation of the Ministry of Finance building, which we delivered ahead of schedule despite challenging conditions.

Over the past 15 years, we have expanded our portfolio to include commercial high-rises, residential estates, and infrastructure projects. We’ve established partnerships with international suppliers and trained a local workforce of over 200 skilled professionals. Our history is defined by resilience, integrity, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.
